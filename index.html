NAVIGATING OUR SUSTAINABLE FUTURE

Sustainability drives everything we do at IWC Schaffhausen. We have been producing watches since 1868 we’ve had over 150 years to perfect our craft, yet we continue to push ourselves. For you. For all of us. Our sustainability journey began over ten years ago, and although we don’t need, nor likely have, a century to perfect our approach, the precision, passion and persistence that go into our watchmaking will continue to drive our sustainability efforts.
